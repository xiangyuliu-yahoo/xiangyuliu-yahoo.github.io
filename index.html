About Me

I am a postdoc researcher at CISPA, hosted by Doreen Riepel. Previously, I was a postdoc at both Georgia Institute of Technology (GT) and Purdue University (2023-2025), hosted by Vassilis Zikas. I received my doctoral degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2023, under the supervision of Shengli Liu. Prior to that, I obtained my master's and bachelor's degrees from Sun Yat-Sen University in 2018 and 2016, respectively, under the supervision by Fangguo Zhang.

I am interested in cryptography and security in general. During my PhD my research mainly focuses on tight security. Currently I am working on advanced signature schemes, blockchain and MPC.
